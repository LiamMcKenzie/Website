5 - friends list (nightcore remix)

In 2022 I was really into hyperpop music. I really wanted to make similar music. This song is heavily inspired by "second hand embarrassment" by underscores.


I spent a long time on this song. In the archive I also included a different version of the song I made in early 2023.


Its called nightcore remix because for this version I performed a specific process to make the song sound like a "nightcore" song from early youtube.

In the original project, I slowed the bpm, and pitched everything down. I then rendered out the slowed version and sped it up in audacity by about 33%. This leads to the song being slightly off key and all the drums are pitched up.

6 - daytripper

This song is named after a mario kart wii vehicle. idk.


I really liked the idea of having a simple song that randomly has a section in the middle that is more intense than the rest. Only to go back to the original simple section.


I used to use crappy earbuds and I'm pretty sure this song sounded better on those.

7 - gender envy

I made this song after I got my first guitar. I had no idea what I was doing so its basically just me playing 3 single notes without any tuning. Even with such a simple melody I still messed up constantly and had to use multiple takes.


I think the mistakes are the best part.


The sampling at the end is terrible.

b-sides

these are songs that I added to this collection when archiving my work. Some were also made in 2023 and 2024.

b1 - look everyone, it's snowing outside

This was made around the same time as the other elegy tracks. It was made from an attempt to make a song by sampling.

At the time I recently got an old electric keyboard my parents bought on facebook marketplace. I was messing around with some of the audio presets and found one called "air". Which I recorded with my phone and sampled for this track. I later realised that this is actually an incredibly famous classical track


I had a rough story in mind when I made this about a catastrophic blizzard that effected a small town.

I sampled lots of random youtube videos about actual blizzards including some news reports and interviews. In retrospect it seems very disrespectful which is why I never released this.
